## The Skyline Project (SF 3D skyline foundation)

The 3D San Francisco skyline in this dashboard — the MapLibre map engine
approach, the CARTO dark basemap styling, and the processed SF building
footprint dataset (`public/data/sf/buildings.geojson`, derived from DataSF
Building Footprints) — is adapted from
[nyc-building-history](https://github.com/perlakay/nyc-building-history)
("The Skyline Project").
